Welcome to TSP Billing Application

This application has been designed with microservice architecture

All the inforations and actions on this dashboard will be coming from the following microservices

  • Tokenleader - For authentication , authorization and storing users details
  • Inventory - For maintaining the inventories
  • Invoice - upload and compare the invoice data with inventory for validation and forward to paypocess
  • paypocess - For updating the payment status
